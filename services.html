Services

In addition to our main service on Sunday evenings, we also have a Compline service on Wednesday nights and “Thursday church” at midday.

Pandemic Update

All services are currently being held online only.

We are working on plans to be able to resume in-person services in the Brent House chapel, following the state, local, diocesan, and University guidelines for gatherings. So that everyone can be included, we will continue to provide for online participation for the forseeable future.

Sunday Evening Prayer: 5:30–6:30 PM

Evening Prayer with sermon and communal reflection, Brent House style.

Wednesday Compline: 8:00–8:30 PM

The last prayers of the day in the monastic tradition, meditative and reflective. Spoken and chanted on alternating weeks, starting 10/7, the Feast of St. Francis.

Thursday Church: 12:00–12:30 PM

We’ll be praying a form of Midday Prayer adapted from the New Zealand prayer book.
